# 2018 - 2021 Triumph Tiger 1200

## CAN IDs

### 0x567 - Handlebar Controls

#### Byte 1

- *Bit 1:* Unknown
- *Bit 2:* Unknown
- *Bit 3:* Joystick Select (high on press)
- *Bit 4:* Unknown
- *Bit 5:* Unknown
- *Bit 6:* Unknown
- *Bit 7:* Unknown
- *Bit 8:* Unknown

#### Byte 2

- *Bit 1:* Unknown
- *Bit 2:* Unknown
- *Bit 3:* Joystick Right (high on press)
- *Bit 4:* Unknown
- *Bit 5:* Unknown
- *Bit 6:* Unknown
- *Bit 7:* Joystick Down (high on press)
- *Bit 8:* Unknown

#### Byte 3

- *Bit 1:* Unknown
- *Bit 2:* Unknown
- *Bit 3:* Indicator / Turn Signal Cancel (high on press)
- *Bit 4:* Unknown
- *Bit 5:* Headlight Pass / Flash (high on press)
- *Bit 6:* Unknown
- *Bit 7:* Unknown
- *Bit 8:* Unknown
